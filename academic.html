Academic Excellence

Saint Claret School offers a rigorous, holistic, and value-based education from Nursery to Class XII under the Meghalaya Board of School Education.

Our Framework

Educational Framework

Fully affiliated with the Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE), our academic structure is designed to nurture intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and character formation.

MBOSE Curriculum

Our curriculum follows the Meghalaya Board of School Education standards from Nursery through Class XII, ensuring academic rigor and board readiness at every stage.

Teaching Methodology

We employ a blend of traditional instruction, multimedia smart classrooms, and experiential learning to foster deep understanding and student engagement.

Grade Levels

We offer classes from Nursery through Class XII (HSSLC Arts stream). Our vertical progression ensures a seamless academic journey for every student.

Subjects & Streams

At the HSSLC level, we offer a specialized Arts stream with History, Political Science, Education, Sociology, Economics, and Computer Science.

Assessment System

Continuous and comprehensive evaluation through periodic tests, term examinations, projects, and co-curricular participation ensures all-round student development.

Academic Calendar

The academic year runs from February to December, divided into two terms. Key milestones include mid-term exams, board preparations, and annual assessments.
