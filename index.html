Our Story

Education as a
Transforming Force

Established by the Claretian Fathers with the aim of providing holistic and quality education to the students of Nongmensong, Saint Claret School draws its spirit from the Claretian vision of social, economic, and spiritual transformation. Today, the Claretian Congregation serves students across 68 countries, carrying that mission forward.

Academic Excellence

MBOSE-affiliated curriculum from Nursery to Class XII, with specialized HSSLC Arts streams.

Holistic Development

Annual Sports Week, Science Fairs, Literary Activities, and the vibrant Expression Club.

Infrastructure

Multimedia classrooms, Science and Computer Labs, and a well-stocked Library.

Our Inspiration

St. Anthony
Mary Claret

Our Patron, St. Anthony Mary Claret (1807–1870), was a 19th-century Spanish prelate and visionary who founded the Congregation of Missionary Sons of the Immaculate Heart of Mary (Claretians). He was a man of action who believed that education is the key to empowering individuals and society.

His Legacy

"A Claretian is a man on fire with love, who spreads its flames wherever he goes; he desires mightily and strives by all means possible to set the whole world on fire with God's love."

Academic Excellence

Educational Framework

Affiliated with the Meghalaya Board of School Education (MBOSE), Saint Claret School provides a rigorous and holistic learning environment.

Nursery

A nurturing play-based discovery environment focusing on language development, motor skills, and social interaction through joyful learning.

LKG & UKG

Structured pre-primary foundations in literacy, numeracy, and environmental awareness, preparing children for the transition to formal schooling.

Classes I – V

Core focus on English, Mathematics, Science/EVS, Social Studies, and Languages (Khasi/Hindi). Introduction to Computer Studies and Moral Science.

Classes VI – VIII

Middle school curriculum deepens academic rigor across all core subjects, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills for secondary preparation.

Classes IX – X

Standardized MBOSE secondary curriculum. Key subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, and Khasi/Alternative English.

HSSLC Preparation

Dedicated mentorship and regular assessments to ensure excellence in the MBOSE Secondary Board Examinations.

Class XI & XII (Arts)

Specialized HSSLC Arts stream offering subjects: History · Political Science · Education · Sociology · Economics · Computer Science.

Co-Compulsory

General English and Environmental Education are mandatory for all Higher Secondary students, aligned with MBOSE regulations.

Science & Computer Labs

Equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, along with a modern IT lab for digital literacy and programming.

Smart Classrooms

Multimedia-enabled learning spaces to enhance visual understanding and student engagement through interactive content.

Library & Prayer Room

A well-stocked school library for knowledge expansion and a dedicated prayer room for spiritual reflection and peace.

Annual Sports Week

Intra and inter-school competitions fostering teamwork, discipline, and physical excellence through track and field events.

Exhibitions & Quizzes

Science and Mathematics exhibitions, literary debates, and General Knowledge quizzes organized to showcase student innovation.

Expression Club

A platform for music, drama, and dance, culminating in the annual Cultural Fiesta celebrating the diverse talents of Claretines.
