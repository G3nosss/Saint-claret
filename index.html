Our Story

About Saint Claret School

Saint Claret School, situated in the scenic hills of Shillong, Meghalaya, was established in 2005 by the Claretian Missionaries — the Congregation of the Missionary Sons of the Immaculate Heart of Mary (CMF). Founded in the spirit of St. Anthony Mary Claret, the school has grown from humble beginnings to one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Located at Umkdait, Nongmynsong, the school serves students from Nursery through Class XII, offering a comprehensive education that balances rigorous academics with character formation. Our school community is diverse, welcoming students from all backgrounds and communities across Shillong and surrounding areas.

Our patron, St. Anthony Mary Claret, was a tireless evangelist, teacher, and reformer whose life embodied service, compassion, and the pursuit of truth. These values permeate every aspect of our school culture — from the classroom to the sports field, from community outreach to the stage.

Our Mission

To provide quality, value-based education rooted in the Claretian tradition — forming young people who are academically strong, morally upright, and socially committed to building a just and compassionate world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Northeast India that holistically transforms each student into a confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizen — inspired by faith, guided by reason, and driven by service.

Our Patron: St. Anthony Mary Claret (1807–1870)

St. Anthony Mary Claret — our founding inspiration — was a Spanish missionary, archbishop, and reformer who devoted his life to spreading education, faith, and justice. Born in Sallent, Spain, he founded the Claretian congregation and served tirelessly as the Archbishop of Cuba. He was canonised in 1950 and remains a model of dedication to learning and service that our school honours every day. His feast day, October 24th, is celebrated as our school's patron feast — a time of prayer, reflection, and community.

Admissions

We welcome students from all backgrounds. Our admissions process is transparent, fair, and focused on finding the right fit for every child.

How to Apply — Step by Step

1
Obtain the Application Form
Prospectus and application forms are available at the school office during working hours (Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 2 PM). Forms can also be downloaded from the school notice board or requested via email.
2
Submit the Completed Application
Complete the form carefully and submit it along with all required documents to the school office. Ensure all photocopies are attested and the application fee is paid at the time of submission.
3
Entrance Assessment & Interview
For Classes I and above, a written assessment and an informal interaction with the student and parents will be scheduled. The assessment evaluates foundational skills and is not meant to be stressful.
4
Admission Confirmation & Fee Payment
Successful candidates will receive an admission letter. Admission is confirmed upon payment of the prescribed tuition and other fees within the stipulated deadline. Welcome to the Saint Claret family!

Documents Required

  • Completed Application Form (2 copies)
  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Previous School Transfer Certificate
  • Last Academic Year Report Card
  • Passport-size Photographs (4 nos.)
  • Aadhar Card / Domicile Certificate
  • Caste / Community Certificate (if applicable)
